ABOUT US


The Causeway Coast Mini Club caters for the mini enthusiasts of the North Coast of Northern Ireland and further afield. The club has monthly meetings, organizes events, attends other mini and old vehicle events and is generally concerned about all things mini/MINI

BECOME A MEMBER


New members are always welcome. Feel free to turn up at one of our meetings, or to come and say "Hi" at any event that you see us at. You can download a club application form using the button on the right.

Meetings are held at the Brown Trout Golf & Country Inn, Aghadowey on the first Tuesday of every month at 8:15pm.
