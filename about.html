About Us

The Causeway Coast Mini Club caters for the mini enthusiasts of the North Coast of Northern Ireland and further afield. The club has monthly meetings, organizes events, attends other mini and old vehicle events and is generally concerned about all things mini/MINI. 

Meetings are held at the Brown Trout Golf and Country Inn, Aghadowey, as shown in the map below. Meetings are held on the first Tuesday of every month at 8:15pm and ew members are always welcome. 

This site is constantly updated so please check back soon. If you need any more information about us, don't hesitate to contact us through the contact us page or you can find us on Facebook.
